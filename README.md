**Customer Churn Analysis – Power BI Dashboard**

**Problem Statement**
Customer churn directly impacts revenue and long-term growth for subscription-based businesses.
This project analyzes a telecom customer churn dataset to identify key drivers of churn, uncover high-risk customer segments, and support data-driven retention strategies using interactive Power BI dashboards.

**Dataset Overview**
- Telecom customer data including demographics, tenure, contract type, payment method, monthly charges, and churn status
- Format: CSV
- Used to simulate real-world customer retention analysis

**Project Objectives**
- Measure overall churn and customer retention
- Identify churn patterns across contract types, payment methods, tenure, and demographics
- Segment customers to highlight high-risk churn groups
- Enable stakeholders to monitor churn using interactive dashboards

**Key KPIs**
- Total Customers
- Churned Customers
- Churn Rate
- Active Customers

**Dashboard Overview**
*Page 1 – Customer & Churn Insights*
- Overall KPI summary
- Churn by Contract Type
- Churn by Gender
- Customer distribution by Payment Method

*Page 2 – Customer Profile Breakdown*
- Senior citizen distribution
- Age group segmentation
- Tenure group analysis
- Contract types and payment methods
- Monthly charges (binned)

**Key Business Insights**
- Month-to-month contracts show the highest churn, indicating lower customer commitment.
- Customers using electronic check payment methods are more likely to churn.
- Short-tenure customers have a significantly higher churn rate.
- Long-term contracts are associated with higher customer retention.
- High monthly charges combined with short tenure increase churn risk.

**Business Recommendations**
- Promote long-term contracts with incentives to reduce churn.
- Encourage customers to switch from high-risk payment methods.
- Focus retention efforts on new and short-tenure customers.
- Implement targeted offers for customers with high monthly charges.

**Tools & Technologies**
- Power BI Desktop

- Data Modeling
- CSV Data Source

**Conclusion**
This project demonstrates the ability to translate customer data into actionable retention insights using Power BI.
It highlights skills in data modeling, KPI creation, and business storytelling, making it relevant for analytics roles focused on customer insights.
